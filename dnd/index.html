No limit to what you can and can't be. You can use homebrew content, modify classes or races to your need. It's al user driven, so if you already have the Player's Handbook you don't need to buy it another time.

With powerful tools to help the Dungeon Master prepare faster and keep track of your campaign. With campaigns, you always have access to your players character sheets and you can provide them with information about the campaign like NPC's and locations.
